## Payout Channels

### Bank Transfers

Bank transfers are the primary payout channel across most supported countries. Processing times vary by country and banking infrastructure.

**Features:**
- Direct bank account transfers
- Support for major banks in each country
- Secure and reliable
- Trackable transactions

### Mobile Money

Mobile money services provide fast, convenient payouts in many African countries. Supported providers vary by region.

**Supported Providers:**
- **East Africa**: M-Pesa, Airtel Money, Tigo Pesa, MTN Mobile Money
- **West Africa**: Orange Money, MTN Mobile Money, Moov Money
- **Central Africa**: MTN Mobile Money, Orange Money, Airtel Money

**Benefits:**
- Instant or near-instant processing
- Wide reach in rural areas
- No bank account required for recipients
- Lower transaction costs

### Regional Payment Networks

Pooler leverages regional payment networks for efficient cross-border transfers:

- **NIP (Nigeria)** - Instant payment system for Nigerian Naira
- **RTGS (Various)** - Real-time gross settlement systems
- **ACH (Various)** - Automated clearing house systems

## Factors Affecting Processing Time

Several factors can affect payout timelines:

### 1. Payment Channel

- **Mobile Money** - Typically fastest (instant to 1 hour)
- **Bank Transfers** - Varies by country (instant to 1 day)
- **Regional Networks** - Depends on network availability

### 2. Amount

- **Small amounts** - Often process faster
- **Large amounts** - May require additional verification, longer processing

### 3. Currency

- **Local currency** - Faster processing
- **Foreign currency** - May require conversion, additional time

### 4. Compliance

- **KYC verification** - May add processing time for first-time recipients
- **Regulatory requirements** - Varies by country
- **Transaction limits** - Large amounts may trigger additional checks

### 5. Business Hours

- **Business days** - Processing typically faster during business days
- **Weekends/Holidays** - May delay processing in some countries

## Getting Coverage Information via API

You can programmatically check coverage using our API:

```bash

  -H "Authorization: Bearer YOUR_API_KEY"
```

The API returns detailed information about:
- Supported countries
- Available payout channels
- Currency support
- Processing timelines
